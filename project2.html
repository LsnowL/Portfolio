This persona design from IAT 333 is to explore design focus for SFU Cheerleading group. It keeps unity on both font and color palette. Also, Icons group composition together and it is easy for viewers to understand. Clear hierchy and separate sections make viewers easy to eye-catching. All of design details contribe to an appealing work.



This persona is based on ethnography data, a group team work. The whole project begins with a survey online to get response from SFU Cheerleading members. By asking general questions, we can get a genernal design focus. Then by interview face to face, we got a deeper thinking about problems that need to be solved. To do an ethnography data slide is a preset by designing a persona.

For this team work, we are three members totally. The most of tasks were to do by our group members togeher. We usually came up with ideas individually in advance, and wrote down on Google document. Then we disccussed together on weekends.
